Frequently Asked Questions

1. Redmi Pad comes preloaded with?

Redmi Pad comes with MIUI 13 coupled with Android 12 out of the box to bring multiple productivities and entertainment-focused improvements. Among these apps, the Notes app has been heavily customized and deeply integrated with third party styluses, incase users want to opt for additional accessories.

2. Does this tablet come with gaming capabilities?

Yes, this tablet is great for casual or intense games since it comes with the segment first, MediaTek Helio G99 processor. This combination of hardware drives the 2.4 million pixels on the screen, at a maximum of 90Hz in supported games such as Altos Odyssey or Altos Adventure (and many others). The quad speaker setup is also quite helpful while gaming to deliver an immersive experience.

3. How many hours of battery life can I expect?

With the segment leading 8000 mAh battery, the Redmi Pad can offer 21+ hours of video playback, or 12+ hours of gaming, or 8+ days of music playback With mixed usage, you can easily expect a full day of usage on a full battery charge

4. What kind of connectivity options are available on this tablet?

Redmi Pad comes with a single USB C slot which can be used for charging as well as for OTG. It also supports WiFi 5 with dual band and Bluetooth 5.3 for seamless connectivity. Through the use of BT 5.3 or the USB port, accessories such as USB adapters, mouse, keyboard, game controllers, audio devices and more can be connected to enhance the user experience.

5. What are the accessories available with the Redmi pad?

Redmi Pad doesn't come with any bundled accessories. However, it is integrated with third party styluses that offer a great tool for productivity, notes taking and creative applications.

6. Any promise for security patch updates?

Redmi Pad offers 3 years of security updates, 2 versions of Android and MIUI updates over a span of three years to aid the overall performance experience.

7. What is the warranty on Redmi Pad?

We are offering 1 year of warranty on product and 6 month warranty on related accessories.

8. How to resolve service queries?

One can avail Walk-in Service for resolving any of their service queries. We have a strong 970+ dedicated ASC Network in India covering 19.5K+ pincodes.

9. Does the Redmi Pad come with USB C charging?

Yes, Redmi Pad supports USB C charging capped at 18W fast charging and comes with a 22.5W in-box charger, which further guarantees endless fun for marathon gaming, movie watching or hours-long photo shooting.

10. Is this tablet ideal for entertainment needs?

Packed with a 26.95cm display with 2K resolution and segment-leading 90Hz refresh rate, the tablet makes an all-around entertainment powerhouse. Powered by Helio G99 and a massive 8000mAh battery, Redmi Pad brings a high-end experience to users with exceptional performance and efficiency. Accredited with low light certification by TÜV Rheinland, in-built SGS eye protection, and powered by Dolby Vision, consumers can enjoy a great audio experience on the immersive quad stereo speakers with Dolby Atmos.
